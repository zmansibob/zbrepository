Food ’n Mood

Some brotherhood
Tribal Spell
to match the smell
of one another
rather
smilingly alike
On foot or bike.
What’s your face?
What’s your race?
Rat o’ Rabbit?
caught in habit!
It’s ok, that’s what it is.
Say “Cheese!”, please?
McRoyal, flesh ’n bread
Wanted reward, alive or dead.
What you see is what you get?
that I wouldn’t bet.
Still having fun?
Gone for another run?
So, have you decided?
Are you undivided?
As in in di vi du al,
sitting by your pal.
When two turns three
You’ll wake up free.
