Vague yet Clear

A cloud of smoke, yet vague.
A smile hidden behind.
A blond hair, the lips.
"I don't want to commit", she said.
Flustered as I was...
Cluster of emotions...
"Can you keep the window open?"
"I need a bit of air"
"Sure." She opened quickly.
As I left out the door.
Traces of silent footsteps.
